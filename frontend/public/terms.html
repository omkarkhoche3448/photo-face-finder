2. Service Description

2.1 What Photo Extractor Does

Photo Extractor is a web application that allows you to:

  • Upload reference photos containing faces you want to find
  • Create shareable links for photo extraction sessions
  • Authorize friends to scan their Google Photos libraries
  • Automatically detect and extract photos matching the reference faces
  • Download matched photos from secure storage

2.2 How It Works

  1. You upload reference photos to create a session
  2. We generate face embeddings (mathematical representations) from these photos
  3. You share a link with friends
  4. Friends authenticate with Google and grant access to their Google Photos
  5. We scan their library to find photos matching your reference faces
  6. Matched photos are extracted and made available for download
